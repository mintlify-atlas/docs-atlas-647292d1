OSGUI is a modern desktop application built on [Tauri v2](https://tauri.app/) that gives you a clean, native UI for `yt-dlp`. Download individual videos or entire playlists, pick your preferred format and quality, and track progress in real time — all without touching the command line.

## Get started in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install dependencies">
    Make sure `yt-dlp` and `ffmpeg` are installed and available on your system `PATH`. See the [dependencies guide](/configuration/dependencies) for platform-specific instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download and launch OSGUI">
    Grab the latest release for your platform from the [GitHub releases page](https://github.com/Ahmedtrooper/osgui/releases) and run the installer. See the full [installation guide](/installation) for details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste a URL and download">
    Open OSGUI, paste a video or playlist URL into the input field, click **Search**, select your format, and start downloading. Follow the [quickstart](/quickstart) for a complete walkthrough.
  </Step>
</Steps>
